About Computer for Schools Kenya

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming donated computers into powerful learning tools for Kenyan schools. Since our inception, we've been committed to bridging the digital divide while promoting environmental sustainability through responsible e-waste management.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide in Kenya by providing refurbished computers to schools and managing e-waste responsibly.

Community Impact

Empowering students and teachers with technology while creating sustainable employment opportunities.

Environmental Care

Reducing electronic waste through refurbishment and proper recycling of unusable components.

Quality Assurance

Every refurbished computer meets our strict quality standards before reaching schools.

Transforming Lives Through Technology

Every donated computer has the potential to change a student's life. We carefully refurbish each device, install educational software, and ensure it reaches schools that need it most.

How We Make a Difference

From donation to deployment, we ensure every computer reaches its full potential in empowering education.

Computer Donation

We accept working and non-working computers from individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Refurbishment

Our skilled technicians repair, upgrade, and install educational software on donated computers.

School Distribution

Refurbished computers are distributed to schools across Kenya, prioritizing underserved areas.

E-Waste Management

Components that cannot be refurbished are recycled responsibly to minimize environmental impact.
